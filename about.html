About Us

A small fast food joint run by the family — built for the people of Haripur Kalan.

How it started

Started small.
Still going strong.

Darjeeling Fast Food started as a small dhaba with a handful of tables and a simple idea — serve fresh, hot food at prices anyone could afford. We've been feeding students, workers, families, and travellers in Haripur Kalan for years.

Our menu is straightforward: momos, rolls, Chinese, thalis, and a proper cup of masala chai. We don't pretend to be anything fancy. We just try to make sure every dish that comes out of our kitchen is worth coming back for.

Everything is cooked fresh every day. Our kitchen opens at 10 AM and the last order goes out at 9:45 PM. No stale food, no shortcuts.
